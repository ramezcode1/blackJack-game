Blackjack

The aim of blackjack is to finish the game with a higher total than that of the dealer, without exceeding 21. Going over 21 is commonly known as ‘busting’ and means an automatic loss.

All number cards (2-10) score the value indicated on them. The face cards (Jack, Queen, King) score 10 points and Ace value is 11 unless this would cause the player to bust, in which case it is worth 1.

You can either ‘hit’ or ‘stand’. If you click ‘hit’, you are given an extra card. if you do not wish to draw any more cards click ‘STAND’. you can ‘HIT’ as many times as you wish, but have to aim not to ‘bust’ (exceed a total of 21).

Good luck!
